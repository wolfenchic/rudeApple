About Us


We are a bespoke catering business based out of Dublin. Our emphasis is on locally sourced produce that delivers the best flavour possible. Our company has catered all types of events, from wedding parties to private dinner parties, corporate functions and pop ups. Our aim is to bring a unique and individual experience to each of our clients.

An alumni of The Ballymaloe Cookery School in 2006, Colm Denham then went on to work in restaurants, catering companies and setting up a fast food business, as well as a 3 year stint in a wine importers, he is particularly keen on wine pairings that best accompany his food. At The Rude Apple we are happy to source great wine at the most competitive prices. We can also provide waiting staff if your event requires it.
